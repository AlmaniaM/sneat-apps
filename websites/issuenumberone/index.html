IssueNumber.One

Continuous Focused Feedback & Improvements process

Do you feel this problem?

  • Too many issues
  • Everything is important
  • Everybody agrees but nobody take actions

How it works?

  • Focus on only what is the most important.
  • Anyone can nominate and have just 1 active issue at a time.
  • Only 1 active most important issue per team. 1 per department. e.t.c.
  • Everyone can support (vote) for just 1 other issue. Either within or outside of own team.
  • For a team all issues are shown & sorted by most importance.
    But only 1 is visible outside of your team.
    Get a consensus on what would it be.
  • The most important issue gets an assignee to drive it and a deadline.
  • The issue gets a simple progress bar.
  • You can check what are the most important issues of teams you are working with.
  • Anyone can see everyone's most important issue. Get to know if you can help.
  • Ask for votes to get support for your most important issue.

Why it works?

  • Surfaces the most important issues and does not let them sink under the load of day-to-day problems.
  • Gets managers to be accountable regards sorting out issues for their reports.
  • Facilitates conversations & collaboration.
  • It's OK to have a long hanging issue as long as it's still the most important one and there is some progress.
