IssueNumber.One

Continuous Focused Feedback & Improvements process

What is a problem?

  • Too many issues
  • Everything is important
  • Everybody agrees but nobody take actions

How it works?

  • Focus on only what is the most important.
  • Only 1 most important issue per person. Only 1 per team. 1 per department. E.t.c.
  • Anyone can nominate just 1 issue and support (vote) for another 1 issue.
  • Issues are sorted by most importance.
  • You can check what are the most important issues of your team or your pears or of teams you are working with.
  • Ask for votes to get support for your most important issue.

Why it works?

  • Surfaces the most important issues and does not let them sink under the load of day-to-day problems.
  • Gets managers to be accountable regards sorting out issues for their reports.
